Pebble Flow has been built using rigorous automotive manufacturing processes. All-aluminum space frame construction. Lightweight composite and sustainable materials. Built-to-last durability that surpasses industry standards. A commitment to making Pebble the safest RV trailer on the road.

Silent HVAC

Whether you want to heat things up or cool things off, simply pre-set the whisper-quiet HVAC with the Pebble App so it’s comfortable as soon as you set up camp. The fully-insulated shell keeps the temperatures just right and the external ultra-high efficiency heat pump keeps things quiet.

Indoor / Outdoor Kitchen

The kitchen features a Wandering Cooktop (a removable indoor/outdoor induction range), a 4-in-1 convection microwave oven, and a full-size fridge. Settle in for a great meal at the built-in dinette for four or pass through the flip-up service window to dine al fresco.

Spa Bathroom

FAQs
Do I need an EV to tow the Pebble Flow?

You don’t need an EV to tow The Pebble Flow.  Pebble Flow’s dual-motor Active Tow Assist System provides a safe and effortless towing experience and works great for gas, hybrid or EVs. As long as your vehicle has a towing capacity of 6,200 lbs, you’ll be able to tow the Pebble Flow.

How do I charge the Pebble Flow?

The Pebble Flow can be charged via  AC/DC charging at public stations, at home, campgrounds, and more. The Pebble Flow’s 1kW integrated solar self-charges on the go and is always topping off its power supply.

Does the Pebble App require the Internet to connect and communicate with the Pebble Flow?

No. All communication necessary for Pebble Flow's functions is kept within a local network, bypassing the need for cloud or internet service providers. This localized communication enables Pebble Flow's features to operate seamlessly, regardless of your location, even in remote areas.

What's included in the Magic Pack

The Magic Pack adds a powerful dual-motor setup and bundles a set of world-first features to the Pebble Flow, including Remote Control, Magic Hitch, Easy Tow and more.

Can I power my home with the Pebble Flow?

Yes. The total battery capacity of the Pebble Flow is 3.5x that of a Tesla Powerwall, making it a great home backup battery in the case of an emergency.
